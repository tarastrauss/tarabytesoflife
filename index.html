About Me


From Sci Fi to Comp Sci:

I'm an LA based web developer from New Jersey who first learned to code in high school. I took a break from programming to attend USC's School of Cinematic Arts and pursue my dreams of working in Science Ficiton. After graduating, I worked as an assistant on Battlestar Galactica and as a Creative Executive at a Elijah Wood's SpectreVision. I rejuvenated my passion for programming this fall at General Assembly's Web Development Immersive and fell in love with coding all over again. I have an unhealthy love for DC superheroes, boardgames and a passion for cooking. Watch out - I can now pun in 4 languages!

Chef's Hat

Chef's Hat is an app geared towards helping users share their secret family recipes with their friends and family. Emphasis was put on user authentication and authorization - don't let those family secrets get out to the public!

ConShareto

ConShareto is a concert-tracking website to help the avid music-lover find and keep track of concerts. This was a group project built by myself and 4 other students from my cohort at General Assembly. My role in the project was primarily working with MongoDB schemas, controllers as well as implementing Google Oauth.


Node.js Express MondgoDB CSS

2048 Crisis

Help Superman recruit other members of the Justice League and fight Lex Luthor before it's too late!
